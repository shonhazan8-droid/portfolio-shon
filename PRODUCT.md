# Product

## Register

brand

## Users

Product and design leaders, hiring managers, and collaborators evaluating Shon Hazan's product design work. They need to understand his judgment, systems thinking, craft, and ability to simplify complex products quickly.

## Product Purpose

Present a focused product design portfolio that turns detailed case studies into evidence of clear thinking, strong execution, and business-aware product decisions. Success means visitors can assess the work confidently, continue to another relevant project, or start a conversation without friction.

## Brand Personality

Calm, precise, and systems-minded. The experience should feel confident without being loud, crafted without decoration, and approachable without losing professional rigor.

## Anti-references

- Generic portfolio templates built from repeated cards and oversized marketing copy.
- Decorative gradients, shadows, glass effects, or rounded containers without a functional role.
- Dense agency-style layouts that compete with the case-study narrative.
- Weak gray-on-gray hierarchy or ambiguous actions.

## Design Principles

1. Make complexity feel structured, not simplified away.
2. Let real work and decisions carry the visual weight.
3. Use hierarchy and spacing before adding decoration.
4. Make every next action obvious and low-friction.
5. Keep the system consistent while allowing each project to retain its own content.

## Accessibility & Inclusion

Target WCAG AA contrast, visible keyboard focus, semantic links and headings, 44px minimum touch targets, responsive reading widths, and reduced-motion fallbacks for non-essential movement.
